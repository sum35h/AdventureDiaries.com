Places

  1. Rock Garden

    The Rock Garden at the Chunnu Summer Falls is a showpiece meant to lure people of Darjeeling after the political agitations disrupted tourism in the 1980s.On the way towards the Rock Garden you will notice the position of the clouds.You will see them somewhere down in the deep gorge ,and as you descend quickly you will see them rising above you. It’s just simply spectacular.

  2. Ganga Maya Park

    About 3km further down is the Gangamaya park. Once you enter the premises of the park, you can find yourself at the foothills of mountains that come from all sides.Yes, this is a valley surrounded by hills and having a natural water stream flowing through it.There are beautiful small bridges built over the stream from where you can look at the crystal clear water flowing over the rocky bed, a beautiful lake, flower gardens ,trees and shrubbery.

  3. Japanese Peace Pagoda

    It is one the Peace Pagodas designed to provide a focus for people of all races and creeds to help unite them in their search for world peace. Like most of the Peace Pagodas, it was built under the guidance of Nichidatsu Fujii (A Buddhist monk from Japan and the founder the the Nipponzan-Myōhōji Buddhist Order).It housed the four avatars of Buddha including Maitreya Buddha.
