PLACES


Gangamaya Park

Gangamaya park is aobut 13km from Darjeeling.It is named after an innocent victim of police firing during the GNLF agitation in 1986.

It meanders down the course of a chanting mountain streams,Clumps of flowering shrubs and trees over humped backed bridges and into a circular lake with a beautiful waterfall.

Sidrapong Hydel Project

Sidrapong Hydroelectric Project Station is located at the foothills of Arya Tea Estat 12km from Darjeeling town.

It is the oldest hydral power station in asia (comissioned on 10 Nov 1897). The station has been accorded cultural heritage status by the central government

Risheehat Tea estate

Risheehat Tea Estate, a garden producing premium Darjeeling Tea. It is located nearly14lm to the south east of Darjeeling town.

It is bounded by Arya Tea Estate in the east and Chongtong Tea Estate in the West. The Himalayas are visible from the town as this garden faces towards Darjeeling town
